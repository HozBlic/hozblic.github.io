You can upload your save file here to extract information about given gifts and donated items. For Windows, usually it's located in this folder:

C:\Users\%USERNAME%\AppData\Local\FieldsOfMistria\saves

AppData is a hidden folder, you must turn on "show hidden files" manually, if you haven't already done so.

Your save file is processed only in your browser and is never uploaded or sent anywhere else.
